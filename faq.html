Frequently Ask Questions

What are your operating hours?

We are open from 2 PM to 11:30 PM, Open everyday.

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, we do not offer delivery services. Customers may place orders online, but they will need to pick them up at the coffee shop and pay upon collection. Delivery might become available soon.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept both cash and online payments, including GCash. For advance orders placed through our website, a 50% downpayment is required to secure your reservation. The remaining balance can be paid upon pick-up at the store. However, if you choose to pay in cash, this option is available when visiting our physical store.

Do you offer any promotions or discounts?

Yes, we have a promotion where you can get a 1 free product for every 5 purchases of any product.

Do you have dine-in options?

As of now, we do not offer dine-in services. All orders are for takeout only. Perhaps we will consider offering dine-in soon.
