About Us

Our Story

Hey there! Uncle's Brew started in 2022, right after the pandemic. The idea was to serve up some amazing milk tea and iced coffee without burning a hole in your pocket. Although we don't offer dine-in services, you can conveniently order online through our website and enjoy our beverages anywhere. Our cozy little spot is at 86B Quezon Street, Tondo, Manila.

Mission & Vision

Mission: At Uncle's Brew, we are committed to creating extraordinary handcrafted beverages—including coffee, milk tea, fruit tea, and frappuccino—that inspire meaningful connections, provide comfort, and support our local community.

Vision: We aspire to establish Uncle's Brew as a vibrant neighborhood café, reflecting resilience and a strong community spirit. Our goal is to grow intentionally while maintaining the cozy and intimate atmosphere that characterized our humble beginnings during the pandemic.

About the Owner

Mr. Chester, a 19-year-old entrepreneur, is the brains behind Uncle's Brew. He's all about crafting budget-friendly, high-quality drinks just for you.
